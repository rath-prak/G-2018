Recovery

Infused organic shampoo

Ghassan Recovery is a reconstructive shampoo with soy proteins, designed for dry, damaged hair. Recovery repairs cuticles with natural soy proteins, keeps it manageable and adds shine. It also has Jojoba seed oil for maximum moisture. Protects hair from UV rays. Anti frizz. Organic ginseng and coconut to help rebuild cuticles and moistures your hair.

  • ingredients

    Key Organic ingredients are Organic Ginseng and Organic Coconut: to rebuild cuticles and moisture hair.

  • Directions for use

    Rinse hair thoroughly, making it soaking wet. Apply very small amount of Recovery on hand, rub between palms then emulsify into hair. Rinse and repeat if required. Follow with either Seal or Masque.

HYDRADERM

Infused organic shampoo
