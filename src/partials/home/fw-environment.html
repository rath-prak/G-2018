Environmentally Friendly

As the world is changing, from people to climate to environment,we are becoming more aware and conscious about our sustainability on this beautiful planet.

We must maintain the Earth that has given us Life, we start by choosing recyclable packaging. The products itself are also bio degradable as they are based from natural ingredients which means any waste broken down will not create toxic or hazardous results.

Ghassan has designed the art and graphics for the range with the thought of simplicity and elegance that would match the crisp, clean lines of the packaging.
